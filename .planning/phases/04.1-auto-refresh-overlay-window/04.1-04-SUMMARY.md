## Decisions Made

**Silent blocking approach:** Per CONTEXT.md specification, implemented blocking without any visual indicators. No "refresh paused" banner or toast notifications - the blocking is completely invisible to users.

**2-second timeout:** Balances responsiveness with data safety. Short enough to resume refresh quickly, long enough to cover brief pauses in typing.

**Event composition:** Both onChange and onFocus trigger blocking. onFocus ensures we block even if user clicks into field without typing, onChange covers all keystrokes.
